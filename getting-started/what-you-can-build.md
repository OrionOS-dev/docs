# What You Can Build

OrionOS enables you to build a wide variety of autonomous AI agents. Here are some examples of what's possible.

## Social Media Agents

### Community Manager Bot

Build agents that actively engage with your community across multiple platforms:

- Respond to mentions and comments
- Share updates and announcements
- Monitor brand sentiment
- Moderate content and enforce rules
- Generate engagement reports

**Example Use Cases:**
- Brand social media presence
- NFT project community management
- DAO governance participation
- Content creator engagement

### Influencer Agent

Create AI influencers with unique personalities that:

- Generate original content (text, images, videos)
- Engage authentically with followers
- Collaborate with other accounts
- Grow organic following
- Monetize through partnerships

## Trading & DeFi Agents

### Automated Trading Bot

Build sophisticated trading agents that:

- Monitor multiple DEXs and CEXs
- Execute arbitrage strategies
- Manage portfolio allocation
- Implement risk management
- Generate performance reports

**Supported Chains:**
- Ethereum & EVM chains
- Solana
- Base
- Polygon

### Yield Farming Optimizer

Create agents that maximize DeFi yields:

- Compare APYs across protocols
- Automatically compound rewards
- Rebalance portfolios
- Monitor liquidity pools
- Execute complex strategies
